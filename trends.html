  1. Computers becoming smaller: Over the years, computers have significantly reduced in size while increasing in power. From large mainframes to portable laptops and smartphones, this trend makes computing more accessible and convenient.
  2. Computers becoming smarter: With advances in artificial intelligence and machine learning, computers are now capable of performing complex tasks, making decisions, and learning from data. This trend is evident in applications like virtual assistants and autonomous vehicles.
  3. Society becoming increasingly reliant on computers: Computers have become essential in almost every aspect of modern life. Most businesses, such as banks, rely on computer systems for operations, and everyday activities like communication, shopping, and entertainment are heavily dependent on computers.
