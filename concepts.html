  1. What exactly is a computer?: A computer is an electronic device that processes data according to a set of instructions. It performs tasks such as calculations, data processing, and internet browsing.
  2. Hardware vs software: Hardware refers to the physical components of a computer, such as the CPU, RAM, and hard drive. Software consists of the programs and operating systems that run on the hardware, enabling it to perform various tasks.
  3. Different types of computers: Computers come in various forms, including personal computers, laptops, tablets, and servers. Each type is designed for specific uses, ranging from general home use to specialized tasks in business and research.
  4. Computer networks and the internet: Computer networks connect multiple computers to share resources and information. The internet is a global network that allows computers worldwide to communicate and access vast amounts of information.
  5. Computers and society: Computers have transformed society by enhancing communication, productivity, and access to information. They are integral in various fields, including education, healthcare, business, and entertainment.
